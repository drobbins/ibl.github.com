Integrative Bioinformatics Laboratory

IBL is a research group within UAB's Dept of Pathology Division of Informatics, at the data driven crossroads of Computational Statistics, Computer Science and Systems Biomedicine. Accordingly, the projects pursued seek translational validation in applications to precision medicine for novel ideas traversing all three fields. In addition to the graduate research level projects, IBL also hosts Pathology Informatics rotations for residents and fellows. This combination of fields and carreer tracks is integrated through immersive problem solving venues including weekly hackatons and software development bootcamps.
